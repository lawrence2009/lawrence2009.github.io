About Me

I am a Filipino professional print and web Multimedia designer with more than eight (8) years of design work experience. I specialize in videos, designing logos, marketing materials, websites, and other print or web related graphics. Business Owner of LensFlare Digital a Multimedia Creative firm with office located in Malaybalay City, Bukidnon, Philippines. We are backed by a team of professionals, and with more than 8 years of combined experience in the business. Our goal is not only to assist and satisfy our clients with our services, but also to provide it in a cost effective and efficient way.
